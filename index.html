How to use the SoundMania App

Click on the icon to play its sound and click on the same icon again to pause its sound.

Rotation of the icon indicates that it's sound is currently being played.

You can mix multiple sounds at once by clicking on their icons without pausing.

To stop a sound from playing infinitely, you must stop it's rotation by clicking on it.

Enjoy !
